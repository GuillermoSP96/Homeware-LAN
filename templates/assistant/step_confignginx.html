Manual way

If the Fancy way goes wrong

Open a terminal on your Raspberry Pi or connect to the Raspberry Pi using SSH and run:

$ cd /etc/nginx/sites-enabled

$ sudo wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kikeelectronico/Homeware-LAN/master/configuration_templates/initial_nginx_config

$ sudo cp initial_nginx_config homeware

$ sudo rm initial_nginx_config

$ sudo nano homeware
